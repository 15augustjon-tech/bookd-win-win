1. Services

Bookd provides invoice factoring services for motor carriers operating under valid FMCSA authority. We purchase your accounts receivable (invoices) from freight brokerage transactions and advance you funds, typically within 48 hours of verification.

2. Fees

Our current standard fee is 1% of the invoice amount. No wire fees, no setup fees, no monthly minimums, no long-term contracts.

Fees are subject to change at our discretion. Any fee changes will apply only to invoices submitted after the effective date of the change. We will notify you of fee changes via email or through our website. Your continued use of our services after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated fee structure.

Individual rates may vary based on factors including but not limited to: invoice volume, broker creditworthiness, and payment history. Contact us for a personalized quote.

By using our services, you agree to:

  • Not submit invoices already pledged to another factor or lender
