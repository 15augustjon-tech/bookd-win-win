Partner Agreement

Terms for freight brokers using Bookd's white-label instant pay platform.

Last updated: January 2025

Note: These terms provide a general overview of the Bookd partnership. Detailed factoring agreements and partner contracts will be provided during onboarding and must be signed before any funding occurs.

1. Partnership Overview

Bookd provides a white-label instant pay platform for freight brokers ("Partner"). Through this partnership, you can offer your truckers instant payment on completed loads under your own brand, while Bookd handles funding and collections behind the scenes.

2. Fee Structure

Bookd charges a flat 2% fee on each transaction. This covers funding, collections, risk, and platform support. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no long-term contracts.

You set your own markup on top of our 2% fee. The difference between what you charge truckers and our 2% is your revenue. Example: You charge truckers 3.5%, you keep 1.5%.

Your earnings are paid after we collect on the receivable from the shipper. Bookd may adjust base fees with 30 days written notice.

3. White-Label License

Partner is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Bookd platform under Partner's branding. You will receive a branded portal featuring your logo, colors, and domain. Your truckers interact exclusively with your branding â€” Bookd remains invisible to them.

Partner may not sublicense, resell, or provide platform access to other brokers. You are responsible for promoting the instant pay service to your truckers. Bookd provides marketing materials and onboarding support upon request.

4. Assignment of Receivables

When a trucker submits an invoice through Partner's portal, the trucker irrevocably assigns and transfers all rights, title, and interest in that receivable to Bookd upon approval. Bookd becomes the owner of the receivable and has the sole right to collect payment from the shipper.

Bookd may notify shippers that receivables have been assigned and payment should be made directly to Bookd.

5. Funding & Collections

Bookd purchases the receivable and funds truckers directly after verifying load completion with you. We then collect payment from the shipper on standard payment terms. Your markup is paid to you after we collect.

Example: Trucker completes $10,000 load. You approve the instant pay request. Bookd purchases the receivable and pays the trucker $9,650 (after 3.5% fee). Once we collect from the shipper, we pay you your $150 markup (1.5%). Bookd keeps $200 (2%).

6. Recourse

This is a recourse factoring arrangement. If the shipper fails to pay within 90 days, or if there is a valid freight claim, chargeback, or dispute, the trucker may be required to repurchase the receivable at full face value plus fees.

Partner may be responsible for advances if Partner approved payment on fraudulent, duplicate, or disputed invoices. Bookd will work with all parties to resolve disputes before pursuing recovery.

7. Trucker Representations

Truckers using the platform represent and warrant that: (a) each invoice is valid and undisputed, (b) services have been fully performed, (c) the receivable has not been previously assigned, factored, or pledged elsewhere, and (d) there are no defenses, offsets, or claims against payment.

8. Partner Responsibilities

As a Partner, you agree to:

  • Verify load completion before approving trucker payment requests
  • Respond to verification requests within 24 hours during business days
  • Notify Bookd immediately of any disputes, claims, or chargebacks
  • Not misrepresent the nature of the instant pay service
  • Maintain accurate records of loads and payments
  • Not circumvent Bookd by directing truckers to alternative funding sources

9. Non-Circumvention

Truckers onboarded through Partner's portal shall not attempt to establish a direct factoring relationship with Bookd outside of the Partner relationship. Partner shall not assign receivables to alternative funding sources for truckers active on the Bookd platform.

10. Fraud Liability

Truckers are solely liable for submitting fraudulent, duplicate, or falsified invoices and may be subject to legal action and immediate termination. Partner is not liable for trucker fraud unless Partner had prior knowledge or was grossly negligent in verification.

11. UCC Filing Rights

Truckers authorize Bookd to file UCC-1 financing statements to perfect its security interest in assigned receivables. This protects Bookd's legal interest in the invoices purchased through the platform.

12. Data Ownership

All transaction data, invoice records, and platform usage data is the property of Bookd. Partner is granted access to view data related to their truckers and transactions only. Partner may not export, sell, or share transaction data with third parties.

13. No Capital Required

Partner does not need to provide any capital or collateral to participate. Bookd funds all instant pay transactions using our own capital and collects directly from shippers.

14. Indemnification

Truckers shall indemnify and hold harmless Bookd and Partner from any claims, losses, or damages arising from breach of representations, invalid invoices, or fraud. Partner shall indemnify Bookd from claims arising from Partner's misrepresentation of the service to truckers.

15. Limitation of Liability

Bookd's liability is limited to the fees earned on any individual transaction. We are not responsible for consequential damages, lost profits, or business interruption arising from platform downtime, declined transactions, or disputes.

16. Termination

Either party may terminate this partnership at any time with 7 days written notice. No termination fees. Upon termination, any outstanding advances become immediately due, and Bookd will complete funding for any in-progress transactions. Truckers may transition to direct Bookd relationship at Bookd's discretion.

17. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information shared during this partnership, including transaction data, pricing details, and business practices. This obligation survives termination.

18. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration in the State of Delaware. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware. Both parties waive the right to a jury trial.

19. Modifications

Bookd may modify these terms with 30 days notice. Continued use of the platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance. If you disagree with changes, you may terminate without penalty.
