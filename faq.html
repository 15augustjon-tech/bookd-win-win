Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing, process, and common questions.

Getting Started
What is invoice factoring? +
We buy your invoice and pay you now. We collect from the broker later. You get cash in 48 hours instead of waiting 45 days.
What do you charge? +
3% flat fee + $25 wire per transfer. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no contracts.
What documents do I need to apply? +
Driver's license, W-9 or EIN letter, MC number, DOT number.
How long does approval take? +
48 hours or less. We verify your authority, insurance, and call you to finalize.
Submitting Invoices
What do I need to submit an invoice? +
Rate confirmation, Bill of Lading (BOL), and Proof of Delivery (POD).
How fast do I get paid? +
Within 48 hours after we verify the load with the broker.
Can I factor just one invoice? +
Yes. No minimums. Factor one or a hundred — your choice.
Do I have to factor all my loads? +
No. Pick which invoices you want to factor. Keep the rest.
Payments & Collections
What if the broker doesn't pay? +
That's our problem. We file claims against their $75,000 surety bond and report them to the FMCSA.
What if the broker says there was damage? +
We verify with the broker BEFORE we fund you. If there's a dispute after, it's covered by your cargo insurance, and our agreement includes recourse — meaning you'd owe us back if the invoice is rejected for a valid claim.
What if the broker says I didn't deliver? +
We don't fund until we have signed POD and broker confirmation. If they confirmed delivery then dispute it later, we go after their bond.
Do you take my whole payment? +
No. We advance you 97% immediately. We collect 100% from the broker and keep the difference as our fee.
